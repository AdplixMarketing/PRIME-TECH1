Most HVAC systems last 15-20 years. Signs it may be time to replace include frequent repairs, rising energy bills, uneven heating/cooling, unusual noises, and the system struggling to maintain comfortable temperatures. Our technicians can assess your system and provide honest recommendations.

We recommend having your HVAC system serviced at least twice a year - once in spring for your cooling system and once in fall for your heating system. Regular maintenance helps prevent breakdowns, improves efficiency, and extends the life of your equipment. We offer maintenance plans to make this easy.

A typical maintenance visit includes a thorough inspection of your HVAC system, cleaning of key components, checking refrigerant levels, testing thermostat calibration, inspecting electrical connections, lubricating moving parts, and checking for any potential issues before they become costly repairs.

There are several ways to improve energy efficiency: regular HVAC maintenance, upgrading to a high-efficiency system, proper insulation, sealing air leaks, using a programmable thermostat, and changing air filters regularly.
